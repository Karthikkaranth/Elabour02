About E-Labour

E-Labour is a digital workforce hiring platform designed to connect skilled workers with people who need their services. The platform helps users easily find workers such as painters, electricians, plumbers, and construction workers.

The main goal of this project is to simplify the process of hiring labor by providing an easy online system where workers can register their skills and customers can hire them quickly and efficiently.
