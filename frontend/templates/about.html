About E-Labour

E-Labour is a digital workforce hiring platform designed to connect skilled workers with people who need their services. This application helps users easily find workers such as painters, electricians, plumbers, and construction workers. The main goal of this project is to simplify the process of hiring labor by providing a simple online platform where workers can register their skills and users can hire them quickly.

This system improves accessibility, saves time, and helps workers find job opportunities while allowing customers to hire trusted labor efficiently.
